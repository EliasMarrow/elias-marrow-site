What we write about

We focus on structural forces that shape outcomes: the incentives that move institutions, the constraints that narrow human choice, and the quiet mechanics of legitimacy.

  • Constraint and behavior under scarcity
  • Rent extraction and invisible value capture
  • Power, enforcement, and outsourced control
  • Systems that present outcomes as “personal failure”
  • What changes outcomes—and what doesn’t

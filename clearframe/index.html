Clearframe

Websites built to be understood.

What Clearframe is

Clearframe designs and installs simple websites for people who care more about clarity than noise. No funnels. No bloat. No optimization theater.

Just structure that holds meaning.

If you already have a site

Clearframe can restructure what exists. This is not a redesign. It is a correction.

This includes reorganizing content so it reads in the right order, reducing cognitive load, removing redundancy and ambiguity, and clarifying what matters and what does not.

If you don’t have a site yet

Clearframe installs a proven one-page structure and populates it with only what is necessary. You are not starting from a blank canvas. You are starting from a known, stable frame.

This keeps cost, time, and complexity low—while avoiding future rework.

What you receive

A complete static website. Clean HTML and CSS. No platform lock-in. No maintenance dependency.

Sites are compatible with GitHub Pages and similar static hosting. You receive something you can keep.

What Clearframe is not

Clearframe is not a marketing agency, a growth service, a funnel builder, or a content mill.

If you are optimizing for engagement, this is not the right tool. If you are optimizing for understanding, it is.
